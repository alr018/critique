Usability Evaluation

Usuability Evaluation of the University of Leicester website through Neilsen's 10 Heuristics

Goal

This website demonstrates the Usability of the University of Leicester website. We used reviewed the site individually through each of Neilsen's 10 Heuristics. Our goal was to find how the website demonstrated positive and negative usability heuristics. Once we identified the pros and cons of the site, we included them in every category of this webpage. Each link contains comments and images of our review of the Leicester website.
